Request: init
+-----------------------------------------------+
| msg-type: init                                |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| id: whatever  (server echos)                  |
+-----------------------------------------------+

Reply: init
+-----------------------------------------------+
| msg-type: init                                |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| id: whatever  (server echos)                  |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| number of chunks                              |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| chunk 1                                       |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| chunk 2                                       |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| ...                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| number of transition groups                   |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| transition group 1                            |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| transition group 2                            |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| ...                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| number of variables                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| variable  1                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| variable  2                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| ...                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| numer of variables (same as above)            |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| type of variable 1                            |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| type of variable 2                            |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| ...                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| number of state labels                        |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| state labels  1                               |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| state labels  2                               |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| ...                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| may-write-matrix (see below)                  |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| must-write-matrix (see below)                 |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| reads-action-matrix (see below)               |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| reads-guard-matrix (see below)                |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| further Matrices ????                         |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| Ltl fomula ????                               |
+-----------------------------------------------+





Matrices format:

+-----------------------------------------------+
| number of rows in matrix                      |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| first transition group                        |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| number of columns in first row                |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| first variable in first row                   |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| second variable in first row                  |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| ...                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| second transition group                       |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| number of columns in second row               |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| first variable in second row                  |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| second variable in second row                 |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| ...                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+
| ... (matrix ends after specified nr. of rows) |
+-----------------------------------------------+
